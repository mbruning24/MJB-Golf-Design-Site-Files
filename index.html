Golf course design is just as much about art as it passion for the game. I have been involved in the game of golf for over 30 years. I grew up on the golf course and I love the game. Golf has evolved a great deal over time, and the way we design golf courses has also evolved. We no longer have the "build it and they will come" mentality. We are more aware of what makes a successful project.

I have taken the experiences that I have learned and applied them to my business model. I don't look to create the longest, most attractive course for the sake of putting it on the resume. I look to create sustainable golf projects, whether it is a new golf development, or a small remodel project, each one is the most important project to my client, and I approach it that way.
