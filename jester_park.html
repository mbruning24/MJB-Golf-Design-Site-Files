Jester Park Golf Course

Granger, IA

Jester Park Golf Course has been renowned as one of the best government owned golf course in the state of Iowa. Set against the Saylorville Lake, the golf course features a rolling topography with large greens and narrow, tree lined fairways.

Green Golf Partners was hired as the management firm and was signed to a ten year contract. Michael Benkusky worked with Green Golf's principal partner, Tom Rodems, on past projects and in 2009 Tom brought Mike in to discuss plans for updating Jester Park.

Seeing that the quality of the bunkers was the main issue, initial plans included bunker renovations. The plans included the rebuilding of existing bunkers, placing new, strategically located bunkers, and eliminating existing bunkers to improve maintenance and playability. New tees and drainage improvements have also been added. The work was completed in three phases with the latest phase completed in Fall 2011.
