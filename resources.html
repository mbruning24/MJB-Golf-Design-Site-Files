Resources

As a member of the American Society of Golf Course Architects (ASGCA), I have a vast inventory of resources to help make your project successful. Whether it is finding the proper materials or getting the right contractor, we can work together to put your team together. Here is a partial list of some of these helpful resources.

  • American Society of Golf Course Architects

  • Golf Course Superintendents Association of America
  • Golf Course Builders Association of America
  • USGA Green Section

I also keep in touch with many of the golf magazines out there to see what is happening in the industry. These prove as valuable tools to keep your project current with the state of the game.

  • Golf Digest
  • Golf Magazine
  • Golf.com
  • Golfweek
  • Links Magazine
  • Golf Course Industry
  • Golf Course Architecture

Finally the ASGCA has affiliations with many groups that help to grow the game of golf. Our current work with the PGA includes "first Links" which provides initial funding for plans to create projects geared for the beginning golfer. Links Across America, through the Wadsworth Charities, can provide funding to non-profit groups to help with construction of these same type of projects. These groups provide help to continue to grow the game.

  • Links Across America
  • PGA of America
  • First Tee
  • Golf 20/20
